Perfect In Our Suffering

7/5/2021 12:00PM

Last night I couldn't sleep as I was laying awake in bed at about 5am. I had all the reasons in the world to feel bad and be upset, which I did physically feel. I said a prayer something like this, "Despite what I'm feeling and no matter how bad anything ever gets I know that you are always good and this suffering I'm currently experiencing is just the price of free will and for true love to be able to exist", and I felt better.

No I didn't magically feel all my pain or weird tiredness or anything leave, but I felt a new feeling rise alongside them that I remain feeling which almost makes the suffering feel like a welcome challenge and reminder of God's goodness.

Scientificially speaking, it could be the love chemical oxytocin rising in my head in harmony with the Christian narrative of perfect love through Jesus that I've believed in and mentally chewed over the past 3 years or so now. Regardless of whatever scientific skeptic explanation could suggest I choose to believe it was God who put it all in motion and it's His love that I'm feeling.
